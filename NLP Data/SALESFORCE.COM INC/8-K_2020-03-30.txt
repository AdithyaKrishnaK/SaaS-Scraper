On March 27, 2020, the Compensation Committee (the “Committee”) of the Board of Directors of salesforce.com, inc. (the “Company”) approved cash bonuses to Mr. Marc Benioff, our principal executive officer, Mr. Mark Hawkins, our principal financial officer, and Messrs.
