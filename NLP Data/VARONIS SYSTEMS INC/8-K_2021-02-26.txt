Item
8.01 – Other Events.
 
On
February 8, 2021, Varonis Systems, Inc. (the “Company”) announced a three-for-one stock split of the Company’s
common stock (the “Stock Split”). The Company is announcing today that the record date of the Stock Split shall be
March 11, 2021, so that each stockholder of record at the close of business on March 11, 2021 will receive two additional shares
of common stock for each then-held share. Trading will begin on a split-adjusted basis on March 15, 2021.
